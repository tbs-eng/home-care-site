Pricing & payment FAQs

How much does senior home care cost in San Diego?
San Diego home care is typically billed hourly, with live-in and 24-hour options for higher-need clients. Your total depends on hours per day, days per week, and care level. Use our free cost calculator for an instant weekly and monthly estimate, then book a free RN assessment to confirm.
Is there a minimum number of hours for home care?
Most San Diego agencies, including Carevider, set a daily minimum so caregivers can deliver safe, meaningful visits. Shorter visits often cost more per hour. During your free assessment we recommend a schedule that fits the client's needs and your budget, with no long-term contract required to begin.
Does insurance or Medicare pay for home care?
Traditional Medicare rarely covers non-medical home care. Long-term care insurance often does, and eligible veterans may qualify for VA benefits like Aid and Attendance. We help families verify coverage, file paperwork, and combine private pay with benefits. See our LTC insurance and VA benefits pages for details.
How is Carevider paid, and are there hidden fees?
We bill a transparent hourly or live-in rate with no hidden assessment, registration, or cancellation surprises. Placement help into assisted living is typically free to families because communities pay our referral fee. Our How We're Paid page explains every revenue source in plain language.
Can I get an estimate before talking to anyone?
Yes. Our online cost calculator gives an instant weekly and monthly estimate based on hours, days, and care level, with no contact information required. When you are ready, a registered nurse provides a free in-home or virtual assessment to confirm the right care plan and exact pricing.
