Why a local cost index matters

National averages can mislead. San Diego's cost of living, caregiver wages, and housing market all push senior-care pricing away from the figures you'll find in nationwide reports. A county-specific index gives families a realistic planning baseline — and a benchmark to judge any quote against.

Use it as a yardstick. If a quote sits far outside these ranges, ask why. Transparent providers can explain exactly what drives their pricing.

From ranges to your real number

The Index shows what care costs across the county; your situation lands somewhere inside those ranges based on care level, hours, and setting. The calculator turns the ranges into a personal estimate, and a free RN assessment turns the estimate into an exact plan and price.

If long-term care insurance or VA benefits apply, your out-of-pocket cost may fall below the private-pay ranges shown here. We verify coverage before care begins.
